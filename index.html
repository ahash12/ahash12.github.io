WHAT I DO

What I am working on.

Gene Editing + Cancer

Cancer is one of the biggest and deadliest diseases facing society and our approach is extremely limited. By leveraging Gene editing, we can provide personalized therapy to patients and get rid of genetic inheritances of the disease.

Nanotechnology

Some diseases are due to micro organisms and cells on a molecular level, yet we are getting rid of them by using medicines that aren't accurate enough. By utilizing nanotechnology, we can precisely deliver medications!!

Cardiovascular Research

Heart attacks and other cardiovascular disease's are serious killers. With many invasive therapies, it can be hard to recover after recovery, but by using nanotech and gene editing, we can get rid of cardiac disease non-invasively!
